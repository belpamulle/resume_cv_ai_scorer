ROLE: Senior Backend Engineer (Python)

ROLE SUMMARY:
We are hiring a senior backend engineer to design and operate high-throughput
services. The ideal candidate is comfortable owning systems end to end, from API
design through deployment and on-call.

MUST-HAVES (heavily penalise if missing):
- 5+ years professional software engineering experience
- Strong Python (production systems, not just scripting)
- REST API design and relational databases (PostgreSQL or similar)
- Cloud experience (AWS preferred) and containerisation (Docker)
- Demonstrated ownership of production services

NICE-TO-HAVES (reward but do not require):
- Infrastructure-as-code (Terraform/CloudFormation)
- Event-driven / streaming systems (Kafka, SQS)
- Experience mentoring engineers
- Open-source contributions

RED FLAGS (call these out explicitly in red_flags):
- Job hopping (multiple stints under ~12 months without explanation)
- Unexplained employment gaps
- Vague, responsibility-free descriptions ("involved in", "helped with")
- Missing the core tech stack (no Python / no cloud)
- Formatting errors or careless presentation in the CV

SCORING GUIDANCE:
- skills_match_score reflects fit against the MUST-HAVES first, then NICE-TO-HAVES.
- Be a strict recruiter. Do not be generous. A score of 90+ means an exceptional,
  clear match across all must-haves with strong nice-to-haves.
